Home/Product/REMIEL Engine

The engine that reads your mind.

REMIEL is the multi-channel analysis core of Beverly Index — an NLP engine that extracts neural oscillation features from human narratives across 8 parallel input channels, including Prosodic Oscillation Analysis for spoken input.

8 Input Channels

Every dimension of human expression.

REMIEL analyzes narratives through eight parallel channels — each targeting a different dimension of cognitive and emotional expression. The first seven process text; the eighth analyzes voice.

Channel 1

Dreams

Highest signal-to-noise ratio. Primary diagnostic window for NOST — theta-dominant content with rich oscillatory markers.

Channel 2

Conflict

Interpersonal conflict narratives reveal attachment patterns, emotional regulation, and relational cognitive architecture.

Channel 3

Emotional

Emotional experience narratives — affect regulation capacity, emotional vocabulary range, and valence processing patterns.

Channel 4

Future

Prospective thinking and planning narratives reveal cognitive style, executive function, and temporal orientation.

Channel 5

Self

Self-referential narratives — identity integration, self-concept coherence, and metacognitive awareness markers.

Channel 6

Speech

Real-time spoken language patterns — processing speed, word-finding, fluency, and spontaneous discourse markers.

Channel 7

Creative

Creative expression and free association — thought flow patterns, associative range, and cognitive flexibility indicators.

Channel 8 — POA

Prosodic Oscillation

Voice and prosody analysis — pitch dynamics, temporal rhythm, energy contour, vocal quality, pause architecture. Enables Text-Voice Divergence Detection.

Prosodic Oscillation Analysis

When what you say contradicts how you say it.

POA — Channel 8 — is what makes REMIEL unique for spoken input. Six acoustic sub-channels detect when the content of speech diverges from the prosodic delivery, enabling Text-Voice Divergence Detection critical for both clinical and forensic applications.

Pitch Dynamics

Fundamental frequency contour, pitch range, and intonation patterns — emotional arousal and cognitive load indicators.

Temporal Rhythm

Speech rate, syllable timing, and rhythmic regularity — processing speed and cognitive fluency markers.

Energy Contour

Amplitude envelope, loudness dynamics, and energy distribution — engagement, affect intensity, and arousal states.

Vocal Quality

Breathiness, creakiness, tremor, and harmonic-to-noise ratio — emotional state and neurological health indicators.

Pause Architecture

Pause duration, placement, and pattern — cognitive retrieval, fabrication markers, and processing efficiency.

Articulation

Precision of speech sounds, coarticulation patterns — motor planning, neurological integrity, and substance effects.
