The Stack

Seven layers of cognitive intelligence.

Every NOUS assessment flows through a seven-layer pipeline — from narrative input through analysis, translation, synthesis, scoring, and immersive visualization.

L2

AAE — Adaptive Assessment Engine

The front door. Universal narrative intake, intelligent routing, and adaptive deep-dive follow-up. Accepts dreams, conflicts, memories, speech — any narrative modality via text or voice.

L2

LOGOS — Cross-Cultural Calibration

Linguistic Origin & Gestural Oscillation System. Builds a Linguistic-Cultural Frame (LCF) for each speaker — 6-layer architecture including tonal disentanglement for 300+ languages. Ensures cultural expression isn't misclassified as pathology.

L3

REMIEL Engine — 8-Channel Analysis

Multi-channel feature extraction across text and voice. Seven textual channels plus Prosodic Oscillation Analysis (POA) for spoken input. Outputs feature vectors and precession tracking data.

L4

R2X Unified Bridge — Translation

REMIEL-to-Everything. Translates feature vectors into visual parameters (DreamWeaver), audio parameters (Memoria Sonata), and diagnostic scoring inputs — all synchronized from a single analysis pass.

L5

DreamWeaver + Memoria Sonata — Synthesis

Parallel output: 2D neural visualization and acoustic sonification. DreamWeaver renders cognitive landscapes in color; Memoria Sonata translates oscillation patterns into sound. Both can feed into Empyrean.

L6

Beverly Index — 6-Component Scoring

Diagnostic Probability, DCI, IDS, Integration Pattern, Clinical Confidence, and Authenticity Index. Continuous scoring across all 12 NOSS sectors with multi-label classification.

L7

Empyrean — 3D Cognitive Topology

Immersive synthesis. 12 NOSS nodes orbiting a theta-gamma coupled core, synchronized with DreamWeaver visuals and Memoria Sonata audio. Psychological Lense-Thirring precession visualized in 3D space.

Assessment Pathways

Four pathways. Every context.

Clinical Intelligence

CI

Full-spectrum diagnostic assessment. NOSS sector activation, Beverly Index scoring, REMIEL agent-guided interpretation, and evidence-based Treatment Intelligence with local and worldwide facility panels.

Forensic Authentication

CSAA

Cognitive Signature Authenticity Assessment. Fabrication detection with zero clinical leakage. Federal forensic report format. Interview Mode with real-time analysis. Authenticity Index 0-100.

Longitudinal Monitoring

VOCA

Vocal monitoring with trajectory visualization, automated changepoint detection, and treatment response tracking. Population health analytics at organizational scale.

Real-Time Call Analysis

RTCA

Live audio analysis of telehealth sessions, crisis calls, and ambient speech. 30-second sliding windows with speaker diarization. The distribution infrastructure that takes NOUS into markets where structured intake is impossible.

Intelligence Modules

Cross-domain intelligence.

Three intelligence modules extend NOUS beyond mental health diagnostics into mortality forecasting, pharmacological response prediction, and cognitive fitness certification.

THANATOS

Mortality Forecasting

Cross-domain mortality risk assessment based on cognitive trajectory analysis. Identifies declining cognitive signatures that correlate with increased mortality risk across conditions.

ORACLE

Pharmacological Response Prediction

Predicts treatment response based on oscillatory signature profiles. Matches cognitive signatures to pharmaceutical outcome databases to forecast which medications are most likely to be effective.

AEGIS

Cognitive Fitness Certification

Point-in-time and continuous cognitive fitness assessment. Issues CERTIFIED (8h), PROVISIONAL, or DENIED status. Integrates with RTCA for continuous monitoring in occupational safety contexts.
