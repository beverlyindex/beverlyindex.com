What Every Analysis Delivers

NOSS Profile

12-sector cognitive signature across Mood, Anxiety, Psychotic, Neurocognitive, Developmental, Optimal, TBI, Neurodegenerative, Captivity Trauma, Substance, Dissociative, and Personality domains.

Beverly Index

A composite cognitive coherence score (0–100) that captures the overall integration state of the cognitive signature. Calibrated against a 15-narrative canonical corpus.

Integration Dynamics

Six formally classified processing patterns (NORM-I, STUCK, HYPER, DISORG, AVOID, DISS-T) with dampening coefficient and predicted Integration Horizon when applicable.

Treatment Intelligence

Evidence-based treatment recommendations and local/worldwide provider panels generated from the cognitive signature, using real geolocation and validated clinical guidelines.

Empyrean Visualization

Three-dimensional cognitive state visualization rendering the 12-sector signature as an interactive spatial model. The visual language of the mind.

VOCA Monitoring

Longitudinal cognitive trajectory tracking across multiple sessions. Automatic precession model fitting, dampening coefficient estimation, and Integration Horizon prediction.

PROFILE MATCHING

Instant archetypal classification against 18 reference profiles. A plain-language clinical summary identifies the closest cognitive pattern. Automatic concern flags alert for CTE-consistent, destabilization, neurodegenerative, and dissociative signatures.

Research and Practice

NOUS is a cognitive intelligence platform available to licensed practitioners for clinical cognitive assessment. Beverly Index LLC also conducts research in partnership with government agencies to extend NOST capabilities into military health, intelligence, and defense applications.

The foundational paper, "Neural Oscillation Signature Theory: Theoretical Foundations for Inferring Cognitive States from Linguistic Output," is published on SSRN (DOI: 10.2139/ssrn.6447802) and under peer review at Frontiers in Psychology. The complete mathematical framework, case studies, and sector lexicon are available at beverly-index.com/case-studies.
