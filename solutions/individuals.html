See what your mind looks like.

Beverly Index lets you experience your own cognitive landscape — turning a simple narrative about a dream, memory, or experience into a visual, auditory, and spatial portrait of your mind's activity.

How It Works

Three steps. Sixty seconds.

1

Share a Story

Tell us about a dream you had, a vivid memory, or just what's on your mind. Type it out or speak it — whatever feels natural. There are no right or wrong answers.

2

Watch It Transform

Our technology analyzes the hidden patterns in your words — the same patterns that neuroscientists study with expensive brain scanners — and translates them into something you can see, hear, and explore.

3

Explore Your Mind

See your cognitive landscape as a flowing visualization (DreamWeaver), hear it as a unique musical composition (Memoria Sonata), or navigate it as a 3D universe (Empyrean).

What This Is — and What It Isn't

The public Beverly Index experience is a window into the science of Computational Psychodynamics — a way to explore what your mind's cognitive patterns look like when translated into visual, audio, and spatial output. It's fascinating, personal, and unique to you.

It is not a diagnostic tool. For clinical-grade assessment with Beverly Index scoring, NOSS classification, and treatment recommendations, you'll want to work with a CNAP-certified practitioner who can guide a full assessment through the NOUS platform.
