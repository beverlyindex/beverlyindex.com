Diagnostic precision meets clinical workflow.

Beverly Index gives mental health professionals a quantified, reproducible cognitive assessment — turning a 60-second narrative into a multi-band neural oscillation profile with clinical-grade scoring, visualization, and treatment intelligence.

Clinical Value

Why clinicians choose Beverly Index.

CI

Quantified Diagnostics

Move beyond subjective impression. The Beverly Index scoring system delivers six-component diagnostic output — Diagnostic Probability, DCI, IDS, Integration Pattern, Confidence, and sector activations — grounded in DSM-5/HiTOP alignment.

60s

60-Second Assessment

A single spoken or written narrative — dream, waking experience, memory, or free association — processed through the REMIEL Engine in under a minute. No equipment, no setup, no technician required.

R2X

Multi-Modal Output

Every assessment generates three modalities: DreamWeaver visual, Memoria Sonata audio, and Empyrean 3D topology — giving you and your patients a tangible experience of the cognitive landscape.

Tx

Treatment Intelligence

Evidence-based treatment recommendations sourced from APA, NICE, and Cochrane guidelines — with a local facility panel using geolocation and a worldwide panel of top protocols for each diagnosed condition.

Clinical Workflow

From narrative to treatment plan in four steps.

01

Capture

Patient shares a narrative via voice or text. AAE guides the session across five modalities. LOGOS applies cultural calibration.

02

Analyze

REMIEL extracts features across 8 input channels including POA voice analysis. R2X reconstructs neural oscillation parameters across all five bands.

03

Score

Beverly Index generates six-component scoring with NOSS sector activations. REMIEL agent provides interpretive guidance.

04

Treat

Treatment Intelligence recommends evidence-based interventions. Local and worldwide facility matching. PDF report generation.
