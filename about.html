Making the invisible visible.

Beverly Index LLC is a health technology company built on a single conviction: that the human mind produces measurable, quantifiable signals — and that those signals can transform how we understand and treat mental health.

Our Story

From the field to the frontier.

Beverly Index began with a question that most neuroscientists take for granted: if neural oscillations shape our thoughts, and our thoughts shape our words — can we recover the oscillations from the words alone?

The answer is Neural Oscillation Signature Theory (NOST) — a framework proposing that human language carries detectable traces of the brain's oscillatory architecture. Dreams, in particular, are generated by the same neural systems that govern waking mental states, making dream narratives an exceptionally rich diagnostic window.

From this foundation, Beverly Index built a complete technology ecosystem — from REMIEL Engine feature extraction through R2X neural reconstruction to the multi-modal outputs of DreamWeaver, Memoria Sonata, and Empyrean. What once required $50,000 in EEG equipment now works from any browser or phone.

The company is headquartered in Sheridan, Wyoming, with research and cultural connections spanning the United States and Italy — where the influence of Basaglia's Democratic Psychiatry movement and Mediterranean approaches to mental health inform the LOGOS cultural calibration system.
